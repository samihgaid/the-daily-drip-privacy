Section 01

Information We Collect

Location Data

The Daily Drip requests access to your device's location solely to retrieve local weather conditions. Location data is:

  • Used only while the app is open (foreground only).
  • Sent to Open-Meteo's API to fetch weather for your coordinates.
  • Never stored on any server operated by The Daily Drip.
  • Never logged, retained, or associated with your identity.
✓ You may deny location permission at any time. The app will still work if you enter a location manually.

No Account or Login Required

The free tier requires no account, email, name, or identifying information. There is no sign-up form.

Crash & Diagnostics Data

The app collects minimal, fully anonymized crash reports (device type, OS version, app version, stack trace). These contain no personally identifiable information.

What We Do NOT Collect

✕ Name or email ✕ Phone number ✕ Browsing history (cross-app) ✕ Browsing history ✕ Contacts or calendar ✕ Photos or media ✕ Background location ✕ Cross-app tracking
Section 02

How We Use Information

  • Providing weather data — coordinates sent to Open-Meteo for local forecasts.
  • Displaying advertisements — Google AdMob serves banner and interstitial ads to free tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data.
  • Processing subscriptions — handled by RevenueCat and Google Play.
  • Improving stability — anonymized crash reports help fix bugs.

We do not sell your data to third parties. Pro subscribers see no advertisements.

Section 03

Local Storage & Device Data

The following is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • User preferences — selected activities, drone config, temperature unit, visible metrics.
  • Flight plans & routes — mission plans, tracked routes, saved locations.
  • Checklists — pre-flight checklist items and completion state.
  • Cached location — last known coordinates for faster app startup.
✓ Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
Section 04

Third-Party Services

Open-Meteo Weather API

Device coordinates are sent to retrieve weather forecasts. No account info is shared. Open-Meteo is free and open-source.

open-meteo.com/en/terms
Google AdMob Advertising

Serves banner and interstitial ads to free tier users. May collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. We request non-personalized ads by default.

policies.google.com/privacy
RevenueCat Subscriptions

Manages subscription entitlements via pseudonymous ID. Does not receive your name, email, or payment details.

revenuecat.com/privacy
Google Play Billing Payments

All payment transactions are handled by Google Play. We never see your payment card details.

policies.google.com/privacy
NOAA / RainViewer Weather Data

NOAA provides geomagnetic (KP Index) data. RainViewer provides precipitation radar tiles. Neither receives personal information.

Section 05

Data Retention

  • On-device data — retained until you uninstall or clear app data.
  • Crash reports — anonymized logs retained up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Subscription records — retained by RevenueCat and Google Play per their policies.
Section 06

Your Rights (GDPR & Beyond)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or a jurisdiction with data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request confirmation and a copy of any personal data we hold.
  • Erasure — request deletion of any personal data.
  • Portability — request data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Restrict processing — in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent — revoke location permission via device settings at any time.

Legal basis: Location data — explicit consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). Crash reporting — legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).

Section 07

Children's Privacy

The Daily Drip is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us at support@thedailydrip.app.

Section 08

Security

  • All API communications encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • API keys use Android's encrypted storage.
  • We do not operate databases containing personal user information.
